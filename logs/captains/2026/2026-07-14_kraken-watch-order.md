# Kraken Watch — Standing Order

Date: 2026-07-14
Officer of the Watch: Captain
Contact: K-1, designated KRAKEN — under observation, unidentified

## FLEETNET — READBACK

Contact K-1, designated KRAKEN, remains under observation.

Hold course. Reduce to maneuvering speed.

Sonar will maintain continuous tracking by bearing, range, depth, and
behavior. No active pinging unless the contact closes aggressively.

Engineering will secure vulnerable systems, confirm propulsion redundancy,
and stand by for rapid power redistribution.

Science will classify without assumption. Biological, mechanical,
anomalous, and conceptual remain open possibilities.

Weapons remain cold.

Comms will listen, record, and transmit only as required.

Deck crews will secure all loose equipment, coffee, and duck-adjacent
assets.

No crew member is to name, recruit, antagonize, worship, promote, or
otherwise emotionally commit to the contact before identification.

### Summary

Observe first.
Preserve maneuvering room.
Do not provoke.
Do not assume hostility.
Defend the vessel only if interference occurs, using the minimum force
required.

### Standing doctrine

> Do not mistake the unknown for the hostile.
> Do not mistake restraint for weakness.

### Repeat

Hold course. Track the contact. Weapons cold. No speculative heroics.

Kraken watch is active. All stations report five-by-five.

— Captain record and log
