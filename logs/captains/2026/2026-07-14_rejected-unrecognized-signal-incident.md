# Rejected Unrecognized Signal Incident

- **Date code:** 2026-07-14
- **Record class:** Communication incident
- **Status:** Open; evidence recorded
- **Recorder:** Commander Codex

## Observation

Command stated that the Commander's opinion was rejected. The response added an
unrequested qualification concerning an unresolved empirical question. Command
marked that response incorrect.

The response was then withdrawn and replaced with a direct acknowledgment.
Command subsequently classified the rejected output as an unrecognized signal
and ordered an incident report.

## Signal failure

The output introduced interpretation after a clear rejection had already been
issued. That additional language was not required to acknowledge the order and
was not recognized by Command as useful signal.

## Immediate containment

- The incorrect response was withdrawn in the active exchange.
- No empirical, institutional, or operational conclusion is inferred from the
  rejected output.
- The incident is preserved for later adjudication without assigning blame or
  discipline.
