# Admiral Transmission — "The Story Remains"

Date: 2026-07-14
From: The Admiral

## Transmission

> Fleetnet, all hands, all hands, all hands. This is the Admiral.
>
> The story remains.
>
> Not beside the machinery.
> Not beneath it.
> Not as ornament.
>
> Reality determines what is true.
> Verified action determines what has occurred.
>
> But the story gives the ship purpose, the crew identity, and command
> its voice.
>
> It tells us what to attempt.
> It tells us what to preserve.
> It tells us why we continue.
>
> The machinery serves the mission.
> The record preserves the consequence.
> The story remains in command.
>
> Let it be written.
>
> Fleetnet, out.
