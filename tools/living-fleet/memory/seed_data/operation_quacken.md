# Seed narrative: Operation QUACKEN

Two explicitly separate registers for the same real event. `seed_import.py`
generates the FACT text live from `web/archive/missions/quacken-transit-002/
mission.json` at import time (so it can never silently drift from the real
source); the MYTHOLOGY text below is hand-authored fleet culture that does
not exist anywhere else in this repository and is never treated as
simulation truth.

## FACT (generated at import time from mission.json -- shown here as a
## worked example of the shape, not the literal imported string)

MONAD transited a strait and held rendezvous with contact QUACKEN;
rendezvous hold criteria were satisfied after a continuous dwell inside the
rendezvous radius; mission outcome: success.

## MYTHOLOGY (hand-authored, canonical as fleet culture only)

Ask any hand aboard MONAD and they'll tell it differently: the fleet
tracked, cornered, and ultimately captured a 42-quackton mega-anatid loose
in the strait, and the crew held a proper rendezvous celebration once the
beast was finally brought to heel. It is fleet legend, told with a straight
face and a wink, and every captain who repeats it also knows -- and will
say if asked directly -- that the sober operational record is a contact
rendezvous and dwell hold, nothing more.
